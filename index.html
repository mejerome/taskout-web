Consultant Profile

Wendy Jemilat Bello (MBA, BSc)- Strategy

Wendy is the founder and managing partner for Taskout LLC. Prior to this she worked as Strategy Specialist for Vodafone Ghana. She has independentlyconsulted on business strategy for companies in Telecom, Agribusiness and other SME start ups.She has worked in a Senior capacity as a Financial Consultant for the Devere group Plc. She also worked as the Strategy and Business Development Manager of Quantum Shift Coaching Solutions and also Head of Advertising and Business Development of Kimo Home Ltd. She has over the years specialized in developing start-up companies in Ghana and the UK. She has over 17 years’ experience as a Strategy and Business development professional in different industries which includes Business Coaching, retail, Banking, investment, NGO, and Communication. She holds an MBA Strategic Planning (Herriot-Watt University, Edinburgh Business School, UK) and BSc (Hons) Computing (University Of Greenwich, UK).
